Product Information

Thanks for purchasing Virtual Chat. This product is a work in progress and will continue to be expanded into the foreseeable future. As with all of our products, we listen to what the customers want, need and implement what all customers, future and present, will ultimately benefit from.

Virtual Chat allows you to create an avatar chat room with no programming knowledge. The future will undoubtedly see multiple chat rooms, more chat options and more content. Though please don't hesitate to share your feedback with us, as this allows us to decide what is most important to add.
